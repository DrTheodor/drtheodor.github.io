# About the project
The Grid SMP is an interesting project. It's a minecraft SMP that has building, roleplaying and RPG all-in-one.
The SMP has custom mobs, items & blocks as well as many different mechanics!
Temperature, magic & spells, beverage brewing & many more.

The Grid SMP utilizes bleeding edge technologies, such as integrating [AI](https://gamejolt.com/p/so-we-thought-making-atthehypersloth-into-a-npc-was-a-good-idea-kiphvqze) & using the [latest](https://modrinth.com/plugin/arte) features available to make players' experience better and more interesting.
